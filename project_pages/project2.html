Swellendam
A client requested a small, inexpensive structure that he and his wife could retire in. The final design of the building is a single-storey structure at 88 square meters. The building is located in a quiet, rural area in Swellendam, Western Cape. The surrounding landscape is mostly wooded, with a few untouched areas. The nearest town is about 20 km away. The building is perfect for a couple who wants to retire in a small, affordable home in a quiet, rural setting. The modern design and comfortable interior make it a great place to relax and enjoy retirement.
