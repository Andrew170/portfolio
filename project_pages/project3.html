Emerald Place
The project consisted of five new residential dwellings built in an estate. The estate is located in a desirable area and the new buildings were popular with buyers. The project was submitted to the city of Cape Town for rezoning and common building line departures this allowed the developer to build the buildings to their full potential. The sale of all five buildings before construction is a strong indication of the demand for this type of housing in the area.
