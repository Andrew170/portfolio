Riverside Oaks
Original building built in 1857, the significant heritage property was sensitively restored to retain many period features combined with discerning contemporary finishes. Two additional modern dwellings were built alongside the original building and was sectional titled. The buildings were sold with private gardens and parking bays.The project was approved in March 2022 and construction completed in 2023. The client had originally envisioned three individual buildings that were not attached and seperated by courtyards.However over time and with a sensitive heritage rating resulted in a rethinking of the original concept.The final result was approved by all ratepayers, heritage of the western cape and stellenbosch municipality.
