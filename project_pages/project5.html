Brand
The Brand Residence is a modern, 5-bedroom residential building that is designed to be the perfect place to entertain guests. The building features a large entertainment space with a fireplace and wet bar. The building is within the clara anne fontein estate, and the the building plan was approved by both the estate architects and the City of Cape Town.
