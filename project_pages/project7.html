Strand
The project consists of a 4 Storey apartment block 30m from Strand beach front and the many amenities of Strand. The 4 storey structure was split up into 24 apartment units with 2-bedroom and 3-bedroom configurations. The developer required maximum return on investment, as a result a high density scheme was put forward for the property.The erf had multiple restrictions attached to the title deed and the cities scheme which all played a role in the final design and complete build.The building is currently fully occupied.
