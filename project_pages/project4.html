Mi Amor
This project involved the construction of a small studio Airbnb unit in Brackenfell, Cape Town. The unit was approved with a common building line departure, which allowed it to be constructed closer to the property line than would normally be allowed.The client requested a cost effective project that was neat and easily maintainable.
